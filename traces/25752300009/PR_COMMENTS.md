## Biological Accuracy

The term accurately represents the molecular function of tethering factors in vesicular transport:
- **PMID:19887069** (Sztul & Lupashin 2009) describes the role of tethering factors in ER-Golgi membrane traffic

The definition correctly captures that tethering:
1. Provides the first point of contact between membranes

3. Occurs prior to docking and fusion
